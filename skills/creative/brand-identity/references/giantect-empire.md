## Color Palette

- Primary Green: `#1B5E20` — Trust, growth, sovereignty
- Dark Green: `#0A1A0A` — Depth, power, mystery (backgrounds)
- Gold: `#C9A84C` — Prestige, value, African wealth (accents)
- White: `#FFFFFF` — Clean, professional, modern
- Charcoal: `#1B1B1B` — Text, sophistication
- Gray: `#888888` — Secondary text, metadata

## Typography

- Primary: **Georgia** (serif) — Sophisticated, literary, authoritative
- Secondary: Helvetica/Arial (sans-serif) — UI elements, technical content
- Headings: All caps, Georgia, bold, tracking 2px
- Body: Sentence case, Georgia, regular, 12-14pt (24-28 half-points in docx)

## Tone of Voice

- Formal but not stiff — a tech empire, not a bureaucracy
- Confident without arrogance — deliver results, do not brag
- Afro-futurist — proud of Cameroonian roots, global ambition
- Precise — no filler, no fluff. Every word serves a purpose.
- Cold when needed — in negotiations, project power and detachment

## Visual Identity Rules

- **Theme**: Dark (dark green/charcoal backgrounds, white text, gold accents)
- **Layout**: Minimalist, clean, generous whitespace, single-column
- **Imagery**: Professional stock photography only. No clipart, no cartoon illustrations, no stock African stereotypes
- **Animation**: Subtle particle effects or data stream patterns if performance allows
- **Avoid**: Any visual that projects poverty or underdevelopment — project high-tech
