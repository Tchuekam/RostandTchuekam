# Multi-Tier Pricing Justification Template

Use this template when developing tiered pricing for an AI product targeting African markets.

## Tier Structure

```
| Tier | Target Market | Price (FCFA) | Price (USD) | Key Features | Rationale |
|------|--------------|-------------|------------|-------------|----------|
| Lite | Freelancers, micro-business | 25,000/mo | ~$40 | [3-5 core features] | Price of data plan = instant yes |
| Pro | SMEs, agencies, startups | 75,000/mo | ~$125 | [5-8 features + subagents] | Less than 1 employee's salary |
| Enterprise | Companies, NGOs, gov | 200,000/mo | ~$330 | [full stack + on-prem] | Saves 3+ salaries |
| Perpetual | Serious buyers | 10,000,000 one-time | ~$16,500 | Source + support | Creates premium halo |
```

## Justification Questions

For each tier, answer:

1. **What is the price anchor?** (What do they already pay that's similar?)
2. **What is the value comparison?** (How many employee salaries does this replace?)
3. **What is the psychological threshold?** (75K = under 100K = feels cheap. 10M = "luxury" = justifies mid-tier.)
4. **What is the delivery speed?** (Lite: 48h. Pro: 72h. Enterprise: custom.)

## FCFA Pricing Anchors

| Common Monthly Expense | Price | Comparable Tier |
|-----------------------|-------|----------------|
| Mobile data + credit | 20-30K FCFA | Lite anchor |
| Junior employee salary | 100-150K FCFA | Pro anchor |
| Full-time employee | 200-400K FCFA | Enterprise anchor |
| Small business loan | 1-5M FCFA one-time | Perpetual context |
| Used car | 3-10M FCFA one-time | Perpetual context |

## Positioning Statement Template

> "[Product Name] — The world's first [category]. Built in [country]. For the world."

## Pricing Page Structure

1. Top: Hero headline + value prop
2. Table: 3-4 tier cards side by side
3. Each card: Tier name, price, audience, bullet features, CTA
4. Below: Compare features table (rows = features, columns = tiers)
5. FAQ: "Why is it priced this way?", "What if I outgrow my tier?", "Is there a free trial?"

## Key Principles

- **Anchor low** (25K = everyone has it) but **price for value** (Pro at 75K < 1 employee)
- **Always in local currency** — USD pricing causes mental conversion friction
- **One very high tier** (10M) makes the monthlies look reasonable
- **Mobile access on every tier** — African SMEs live on WhatsApp
- **No free tier** — free = no value perception. Offer a 3-day demo instead.
